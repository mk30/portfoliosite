I live in Oakland, CA. I make mosaics and digital art.

In my mosaic work I primarily work with broken car glass (aka tempered glass), although I also make mosaics out of more "traditional" materials (ceramic and glazed tile, natural stone, and found objects). A number of my mosaics have also included light-up components and I intend to work more electronics into my work in the future.

In my digital art, I enjoy creating digital collages and web comics.

I am also involved with the Sudo Room hackerspace and the free programming school Cyber Wizard Institute.

You can contact me at marina.kukso@gmail.com or on twitter at @marinakukso. I am a prompt replier and am available for commission on any project - big or small. In addition to solo work, I am also available to work on any phase of collaborative and community projects and to teach mosaic techniques to adults and children. Whether you know exactly what you want or you have a general idea and would like to work together to flesh it out, I'm happy to chat with you.
