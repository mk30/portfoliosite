Growing out of my love for the mosaics of Oakland, CA (where I'm based), I began creating mosaics in 2013. Although I've worked with many different materials (ceramic tile, glass tile, natural stone, broken dishes, mirror, and other found objects), lately I've mostly been working with broken car glass found on the street or donated by the kind people at AutoGlassNow in West Oakland.

If you'd like to learn more about my technique or have any other questions, please contact me at marina.kukso@gmail.com. Also I'm available for commissioned work, consultation, group projects, instruction, and all kinds of collaborations (multimedia installation? ^-^). My work is available for exhibition and for sale. Not all of the work shown here is available though, so please email me to discuss particulars.
