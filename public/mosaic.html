I began creating mosaics in 2013, inspired by the mosaics of Oakland, CA (where I lived at the time).

I've worked with many different materials including ceramic and glass tile, natural stone, broken dishes, mirror, broken car glass, and other found objects. I love using found and discarded materials because they are beautiful, plentiful, and often free!

I'm available for commissioned work, consultation, instruction, and all kinds of collaborations. My work is available for exhibition and for sale.
